Branding Ideas

Fonts

I will be using Garamond Premier Pro for headings and options and Sans Serif for paragraph text. Garamond adds an "old story book" stylistic elements in the text, which may make it harder to read, whereas sans serif is very simple and well-spaced. Example below:

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.

Colour Palette

#000000

#ffffff

#CCCCCC

Most aspects of the site will be done with black and white in terms of text and elements, in order to allow the site to have a storybook/novel-like theme. Light grey is used for small accents and focused buttons. The site is meant to look very dark to give more emotional value to the design. The site should look modern with its elements and art, but also look like an old storybook.
