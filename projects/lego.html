LEGO: mars perseverance rover AR

Role

One of two designers, overseen by a creative director.

Tools

Figma, Miro, Jira

Overview & impact

This project was completed in collaboration with LEGO, as one of their LEGO® Technic™ AR+ experiences. We were tasked to create an augmented reality experience that both entertained and educated users on the valuable insights the Mars Perseverance Rover provides.

As of August 2025:

  • 500,000+ global downloads
  • 4.7 stars on the Apple App Store
  • 4.4 stars on the Google Play Store

The challenge

As a remotely controlled vehicle, it is not easy to convey the experience to users down on earth. We were challenged to educate the user but not overwhelm them with info, and make it a natural learning process through minigames. Additionally, given that the experience was to be delivered fully in AR, we needed to ensure the user was fully immersed. This meant minimal UI obstruction of the AR camera and ensuring that the user could always see the camera feed in the background of menus and dialogs.

To take advantage of AR technology, we came up with a few activities:

  1. A search mission minigame for the Ingenuity drone paired with the Perseverance, powered by arcade-like controls on screen.
  2. An object scanning minigame to introduce several of the Perseverance's different cameras and sensors.
  3. Interactive menus that follow the Perseverance's route on Mars, and AR-mapped menus on the user's LEGO Perseverance model to learn more about the vehicle and real-time updates.
